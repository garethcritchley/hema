Blossfectern for women and girls

It’s time to Fight Like a Girl!

Class Background

Looking to try something new, explore historical fighting techniques and test your skills with a sword? The School of Arms and Armour will be running a six-week introduction to Blossfectern (unarmoured combat) for women and girls.

It will cover the basics of longsword, dagger and wrestling. Following the course, students will join the club’s beginner’s class for the last four weeks of term.

Details and Registration

Classes start 20 April 2026
Time: Mondays 6:30-8:30pm
Where: Mount Stromlo High School, 220 Badimara St, Waramanga
Cost: $110 per term.(Includes six-week Fight Like a Girl course and four weeks with the beginners’ class)

Register here:
https://app.classmanager.com/trials/school-of-arms-and-armour/term-2-fight-like-a-girl-3

Get in touch

If you are interested in attending a class, we are happy for you to come along to a few sessions at the beginning of each term free of charge while you decide if you want to join the school.

Please let us know beforehand if you intend to come, as space is limited.
