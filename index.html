Welcome to the School of Arms and Armour

The School of Arms and Armour is a group located in Canberra dedicated to the study and teaching of the martial arts of medieval Europe. We use the surviving medieval fencing manuals to try to learn the ways of combat practiced by medieval knights, both in and out of armour.


The Three Schools of Combat

Harnishfecten

Fighting in Armour

Learn how to wield a pollaxe, spear or sword. Wear full steel armour and mail to reconstruct how medieval knights would have fought in duels and tournaments.

Learn more

Blossfecten

Fighting out of Armour

The long sword in two hands is a fast and elegant weapon and lends itself to an array of sophisticated and subtle techniques. Dagger and wrestling also round out our curriculum.

Learn more

Rossfecten

Fighting on Horseback

For practical reasons the School does not cover any Rossfecten material. However, if you wish to learn skill at arms on horseback or jousting, contact our friends at Riding With Sticks.
