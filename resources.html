Books

We use the books published by Hugh Knight. The full list is here.

The Knightly Art of the Longsword. This is the main book for longsword. You should get your own copy and I would recommend getting the spiral bound version.

Die Schlachtschule Fechtbuch. This an aide mémoire for students of our school to learn all of the techniques we practice.

There are a number of other books and you should be getting them as you begin working with that particular weapon form.

Buying Guide

Attached is a list of Vendors for various pieces of equipment that you will eventually need on your journey. It is recommended for beginners to not invest in any equipment other than basic kit (Wasters, Gloves etc) until you have a better understanding of what you are looking for.

For those newer to the school, remember to check in with one of the senior members of the School Armorer to see if what you are planning to purchase is suitable for use at the school.

Get in touch

If you are interested in attending a class, we are happy for you to come along to a few sessions at the beginning of each term free of charge while you decide if you want to join the school.

Please let us know beforehand if you intend to come, as space is limited.
